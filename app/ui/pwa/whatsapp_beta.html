What works

  • Incoming WhatsApp messages can chat with your AI and trigger the same agent and task logic as Telegram.
  • Only allowlisted senders are processed, and group chats are ignored for safety.
  • Replies are delayed slightly to look less robotic and to reduce risk.
  • The connected WhatsApp account is the real sender. The sender phone field here helps you manage and document that profile.
